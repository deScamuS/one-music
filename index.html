About

I can still remember when I touched the ivory white keys at the age of six. A beautiful sound coming out of the satin black grand piano...the pedal below allowing notes to flow together creating a gorgeous melody. But I did not know how to make music. My yearning to master the piano was so strong that I practiced for hours a day. By the age of twelve I was playing Chopin, Mozart, DEBUSSY. It was at that times I fell in love with deep romantic classical pieces. Today it grows with any song that grasps and pulls at my soul. Without the piano I would be lost. It grounds me, it makes me feel alive....it keeps me going.
